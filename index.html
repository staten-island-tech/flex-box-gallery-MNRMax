Top 9 Minecraft Bridging Methods

(I don't know why I chose this, I had no ideas)

Andromeda

Andromeda bridging is the fastest bridging method in the game, but it has never been succesfully executed for more than 2 blocks. However, it is theoretically possible.

Moonwalk

Moonwalk is the easiest no-shft bridge to learn. It is also one of the best looking methods.

Ninja Bridge

Ninja bridge, also known as speed bridging, is the first bridging method people learn after shift bridging. It is faster than shift bridge but still one of the slowest methods on tis list.

Telly Bridge

The second fastest bridging method on this list. It is not very common to be good at this bridge and there are probably less than 1000 people that are consistent with it.

Derp Bridge

While not being very practical, it is a cool bridge that is used purely to show off.

Shift Bridge

Shift bridge is the method every bad player uses. It is the slowest method on this list by far.

God Bridge

God bridging the the most know no-shift bridging method. It is not too difucult to learn but when you are consistent it is very fast and useful.

Breezily

Breezly bridging is not very practical but it is the best looking bridging method on this list. It is overall very clean and is easy to learn.

Jump Bridge

Jump Bridging is not very practical, and it is pretty slow, but it can be used just to show off.
